Ninja GR100 Sizzle Smokeless Indoor Grill

Meet the Ninja Sizzle, the compact cooking system that high-heat grills your favorite meals right from your countertop. Reaching temps up to 500°F, achieve the perfect char-grilled marks with direct, edge-to-edge even heat. Satisfy any meal craving using the interchangeable grill, from fluffy pancakes to tender chicken breasts that the whole family will enjoy.


Product Features:

  • INTERCHANGEABLE GRILL: From char-grilled strip steaks to sizzling fajita veggies, unlock delicious flavors any time of the day using the interchangeable grill.
  • 500°F MAX HEAT: Reach temperatures of up to 500°F for high-heat grilling and searing and get better char-grilled marks right from your kitchen. (vs. PowerXL Smokeless Grill Elite)
  • EDGE-TO-EDGE COOKING: No hot spots. No cold spots. Just even, edge-to-edge heating for perfectly cooked dishes every time.
  • SMOKELESS GRILL: The perforated mesh lid eliminates smoke from your kitchen and reduces splatter, and the plates are equipped with high walls and grease catches for a pleasant cooking experience.
  • FAMILY-SIZED CAPACITY: The 14’’ grill plates fit a half dozen burgers and pancakes to feed a family of 4-6 people.
  • HASSLE-FREE CLEANUP: The removable grill plates are nonstick and easy to clean, and the perforated mesh lid is detachable and dishwasher-safe.
  • COOK HOOD UP OR HOOD DOWN: Give a blast of concentrated high-heat with the hood up or drop the lid to retain heat and melt toppings.
  • WHAT’S INCLUDED: Ninja Sizzle Smokeless Indoor Grill , 14’’ Nonstick Grill Plate, & Quick Start Guide with 10 Recipes.
